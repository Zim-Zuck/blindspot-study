RoomRhythm Visualizer — Critical Path Prototype

homography warp · realism layers · nothing else

1 — Wall photo

Use a real room photo. Angled walls, shadows, furniture — the harder the better.

2 — Place corners

Tap 4 points where the poster's corners should sit: roughly top-left → top-right → bottom-right → bottom-left. Drag any handle to adjust.

3 — Realism layers

Toggle layers off one at a time to see what each contributes. "All off" = naive paste, your control sample.

4 — Render

Waiting for wall photo.

Squint test — score it honestly

▢ Perspective: do all four edges sit flat on the wall plane?
▢ Scale: is the poster a believable physical size?
▢ Edges: any bright halo or razor-sharp cut line?
▢ Lighting: does brightness vary the way the wall's does?
▢ Shadow: does it look attached, not floating?
▢ Texture: does it look pasted-on when zoomed in?
